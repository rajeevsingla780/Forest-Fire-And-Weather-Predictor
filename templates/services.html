What do we do?

We provide updates from around the world and inform you regarding any unforeseen events that may happen around you

Intervention

We identify and intervene in areas where there is growing threat of further degradation. Forest Departments are communicated new developments reported on our systems immediately.

Tropical Focus

Our geographical focus is the tropics where forests and trees are more efficient at cooling temperatures through evapotranspiration and cloud reflectivity.

Collaborate

Our mission is to build upon corporate and scientific partnerships and provide the vision, the resources and the knowledge to empower communities to restore their forests and scale their impact.

Responsiveness

We stay in touch with your local authorities constantly to make them aware of abrupt climatic changes

Testing for Perfection

On-site drills and safety measures are regularly executed to ensure the proper training and technique.

Community Engagement

We work with local communities to give them ownership, leverage local knowledge and enhance environmental stewardship capacity.
